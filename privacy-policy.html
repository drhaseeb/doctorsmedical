PRIVACY POLICY

Introduction

Welcome to our website. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use our services. By accessing our website, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Information We Collect:

  • Personal Information

    We collect personal information when you:

    • Schedule appointments
    • Make payments
    • Contact us via phone or email
    • Consult a doctor online

    The information we collect may include:

    • Name
    • Contact details (Name, Email, Phone etc)
    • Medical History
    • Payment Information
  • Non-Personal Information

    We collect some non-personal information like:

    • Browser type
    • IP address
    • Device information
    • Website usage data (analytics)

Usage of Data

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Scheduling appointments
  • Processing payments
  • Providing medical services
  • Improving our website and services

Data Security

We take data security seriously. Your information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel. We use encryption, firewalls, and other security measures to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Calendar

    When you schedule appointments, we integrate with Google Calendar. Your appointment details recorded by Google for scheduling purposes. You may visit Google's privacy policy for further details on how your information is processed.

  • Stripe

    For payment processing, we use Stripe. Your payment information is securely processed by Stripe. You may visit Stripe's privacy policy for further details on how your information is processed.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Check our website for the latest version.
